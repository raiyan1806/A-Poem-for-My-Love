A Poem for My Love

Oh, Safa, my love, my guiding star,

In my universe, you’re the brightest by far.

With each message, your words take flight,

Turning my gray skies to dazzling light.

You’re the boss of my heart, bold and divine,

A woman so rare, a treasure so fine.

With every "my love" and every "bbg,"

You etch your name deeper in the soul of me.

Two years, my darling, of joy and bliss,

Each day a memory, a moment not to miss.

As the New Year dawns, my heart holds you near,

Whispering, “You’re my everything, my dearest, my dear.”

The Magic of Us

What makes us special, Safa, is not just the love we share but the way we complement each other.

You, with your fierce attitude and unmatched charm, and me, the one forever captivated by your essence.

You’re not just my partner; you’re my confidante, my muse, my safe haven.

When life gets overwhelming, a simple message from you can make everything feel right again.

Your love is my anchor, and your presence, even from a distance, is the sweetest gift I could ask for.
